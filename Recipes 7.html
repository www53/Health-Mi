Kitty's Tasty Rabbit and Chicken Dinner

Meat / Challenge

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 g vitamin E powder
  • 1/16g B12 powder (crushed tablets also work)
  • 2g taurine
  • 3/4 teaspoon of iodised salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 separated eggs (lightly cook the whites but leave the yolk raw)
  • 350g boneless chicken
  • 1.3kg rabbit thigh (including skin and bone)
  • Liver (as much or little as you want)

Instructions:

    1.Put the rabbit thighs, pieces and liver through the grinder so that itâ€™s finely ground.

    2.Dissolve the salt and supplement in the water then add to the meat mixture and stir well.

    3.Portion into small containers and freeze or refrigerate until needed.
