Chicken and Carrot Mash

Meat/ Easy

Ingredients:

  • 70g chicken breast
  • 20g carrot mash

Instructions:

    1.Steam the chicken breast and shred it into small pieces.

    2.Cook the carrot until soft and mash it.

    3.Combine the chicken and carrot mash.
