Tasty Tuna and Cornmeal Bakes

Fish / Moderate

Ingredients:

  • 170g undrained tuna
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Cookie/biscuit cutter
  • Baking paper

Instructions:

    1.Preheat oven to 170Â°C.

    2.Place all ingredients into mixing bowl, stir well until soft dough forms.

    3.Let dough rest for 10 minutes.

    4.Cover large surface with cornmeal and roll dough out.

    5.Use either a small cookie/biscuit cutter or knife to cut into shapes.

    6.Place on baking paper and bake for 15-20 minutes.

    7.Let cool and serve.
