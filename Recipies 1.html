Organic Treats with Spinach and Chicken 2

Fish / Moderate

Ingredients:

  • 425g can of tuna, drained
  • 1 tablespoon dried catnip
  • 1-2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup coconut flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp extra light olive oil

Instructions:

    1.Preheat your oven to 180°C (160°C fan-forced).

    2.Combine all ingredients in a food processor.

    3.Pinch the mixture into small crouton shapes, and place on a biscuit tray lined with baking paper.

    4.Bake croutons in the oven for approximately 12-15 minutes until they have browned.

    5.Allow them to cool completely before feeding them to your feline.

    6.Store in an airtight container in your fridge, and use within 7 days.
